- **1st June 2025**: Challenge launch with training/development datasets; baseline; rules and documentation.
- **1st October 2025**: Release of evaluation data and opening of submission window.
- **1st November 2025**: Submission deadline. All entrants must have submitted their predictions plus a draft of their technical report.

If CLIP2 is accepted as an ICASSP SP Grand Challenge (decision in July)

- **1st December 2025**: Top teams invited to submit 2 page paper for ICASSP
- **January 2026**: ICASSP camera-ready 2-page papers due.
- **16th-21th May 2027**: Session during ICASSP 2027, Toronto, Canada.

If CLIP2 doesn't become an ICASSP SP Grand Challenge, we'll organise a separate Cadenza workshop for entrants.
