## Task 1: Improving Lyrics Intelligibility

The table below reports:

1. HAAQI: Average HAAQI of left and right ear
2. Corr.: Percentage of correct transcribed words in better ear.
3. Score: HAAQI and Correctness weighted using alpha
4. Z Norm: Z normalized HAAQI and Correctness.
5. BAQ: Audio quality given by listener panel
6. Intell: Intelligibility score given by listener panel
7. Score LI: BAQ and Intelligibility weighted using alpha
8. Z Norm LI: Z normalized BAQ and Intelligibility
