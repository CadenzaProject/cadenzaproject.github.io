Registration

Sign up to our Google group for alerts about the challenges and to help shape the challenges. To enter the challenge, teams are required to register using the form below. Please submit one form per team, providing a single contact email address. Once you have registered, you will receive an email confirmation with a team ID. When the submission date approaches, you will be sent an individualised link to a OneDrive for submitting materials.
